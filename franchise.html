Frequently Asked Questions

What is the initial investment for a franchise?

The initial investment varies depending on the location and size of the store. Detailed financial requirements will be discussed during our initial meetings.

Do I need previous experience in retail or supermarkets to own a franchise?

Yes, we require our franchisees to have at least 5 years of management experience. This doesn't necessarily have to be in retail or supermarkets, but experience in these fields can be beneficial. Regardless of your background, our comprehensive training program will equip you with the knowledge and skills needed to operate your Great Asian Market franchise successfully.

What kind of support can I expect as a franchisee?

We provide continuous support in various areas including marketing, operations, customer service, and more. Our success is tied to your success, so we are invested in providing the support you need.

How do I get started with the franchising process?

Start by filling out the inquiry form below. Our team will then contact you to discuss the opportunity in more detail.
